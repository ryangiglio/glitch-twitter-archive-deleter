Twitter Archive Deleter

Instructions - Downloading your Twitter Archive

  1. Log in to Twitter

  2. Go to Your Account settings (Settings and Privacy in the avatar menu) https://twitter.com/settings/account

  3. Scroll down to "Your Tweet archive" and click the "Request your archive" button.

    You will see a message that Twitter will email you a link and it may take a little while. In my experience it usually takes a half hour to an hour, but I think it depends on how many Tweets you have. The more the longer.

  4. When you get the email, click the "Download Now" button.

  5. It'll bring you to a page with a Download button. Click it!

  6. Unzip the download In the root of the zip there will be a tweets.csv file. That's what you need!

  7. Drag and drop the csv to file into Glitch to upload it.
