Instructions - Remixing this app on Glitch

Glitch is a platform that lets you spin off copies of other people's code and modify/run them yourself. In the case of this app, that will let you upload your archive and set up your credentials inside a private copy that you control. While Glitch does let you do this without registering, I recommend you do anyway because that will allow the app to save your info and pick up where it left off in case of an issue.
